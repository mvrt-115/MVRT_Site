After a great competition in Utah, MVRT students woke up on Sunday to find that our robot trailer was no longer attached to our mentor’s truck. This trailer was completely full, ready to transport our robot, spare and replacement parts, tools, and full pit setup back to California. While MVRT leadership has been working with the police in Utah to locate the missing trailer, it’s likely that most, if not all, of what was taken will not be recovered. Although we’re devastated by the theft, we are determined to overcome this obstacle and compete again this season. MVRT is a family of strong and resilient students, with the support of our sponsors and our community we’re confident that we will recover in time for the Idaho Regional later this month.

For anyone interested in helping our team, please consider donating to us through our school associated student body store here.

Donations through this link are 501(c)(3) eligible donations and are tax deductible! Any donation is greatly appreciated, please consider sharing this link as well.

If you're interested in helping the team by donating tools or materials, please reach out to us at mvrt@mvrt.com. We are currently putting together a list of parts and tools needed to rebuild our robot. So far, we have been floored by the outpouring of support from the FIRST and local community. Thank you to everyone and every team that has already reached out to us.
