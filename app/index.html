Our Team

MVRT is a student-run high school robotics team that participates in the annual FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC).

FIRST

FIRST is an organization whose goal is to spread STEM values, and inspire and enable students to be innovative thinkers. Their programs also challenge students's teamworking and problem-solving abilities, giving them a "sneak peek" into the professional world.

  • More about FIRST

The Competition

Every year the FIRST organization holds the FIRST robotics competition, a high school robotics competition in which each team must build a robot that can participate in that year's robot game. The competition is composed of a score-based game where alliances, composed of three teams each, compete against each other in a tournament-style bracket.

Outreach

In addition to building and programming robots, MVRT also actively reaches out to others by constantly giving back to the community, and by spreading STEM and FIRST values.
